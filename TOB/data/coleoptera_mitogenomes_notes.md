- 112 mitogenome species matched TOB nuclear winners (exact name match; synonyms not resolved)
- Name-matching is conservative — some matches may be missed due to subspecies names or synonymy

- These multi-submission species skew record counts vs. unique species counts; use unique species counts for diversity assessment
